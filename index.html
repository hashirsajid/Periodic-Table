Important Of Periodic table

5 Important Point


  1. The periodic table is organized in increasing order of atomic number (number of protons).

  2. Groups (columns) have elements with similar chemical properties. Periods (rows) show trends in properties like atomic size and reactivity.

  3. Most elements are metals (left side), nonmetals are on the right, and metalloids lie in-between.

  4. Trends like electronegativity, atomic radius, and ionization energy repeat periodically across the table.

  5. The periodic table helps you predict reactions, bonding types, and properties of unknown elements.
